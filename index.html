Event Information

We are excited to present the Fourth Annual Glowing Smiles 5K Run/Walk and Kids Dash, which will be held on Saturday, May 7, 2016 at Orchid Heights Park. Adults and children of all ages, from Middleton and the State of Wisconsin, will join together in memory of Lina Vergara and to support the Lina Vergara Memorial Scholarship Fundraiser.

Race Day

Be on time, so you don't miss all the fun!

Starting at 9:00 AM, we will have awesome activities, including a raffle for the race participants, important announcements, refreshments, warm up exercises, face painting, balloon animals and the Kids Dash. Make sure you leave home early, so you can enjoy this day with your family and friends!

Schedule

  • 9:15 AM – Kids Dash
  • 10:00 AM – 5k Run/Walk

Special Hotel Rate for our Participants

For those who live in another city and are traveling to Middleton for our race, you can use the special “friends and family rate” of $59.00 per night to stay at the Fairfield Inn and Suites (standard room) or $69.00 at the Residence Inn (studio suite), both located in Middleton.

About

The Glowing Smiles 5k run / walk and kids fun dash is a fundraiser for the Lina Vergara Memorial Scholarship. Lina had a passion for teaching and caring for everyone’s needs. The scholarship will benefit elementary education students at the University of Wisconsin Oshkosh, who share Lina’s desire to teach and assist others.

In addition, the Lina Vergara Memorial Scholarship is also available for students at the Middleton High School, who are planning to attend a 4-year institution in pursuit of a degree in education.

We want to thank previous participants and sponsors from the bottom of our hearts for your tremendous support. Your contribution has not only been a tribute to our daughter and sister Lina, but will also benefit future teachers.

Thanks to your generous donation, we were able to award the 2015 Lina Vergara Memorial Endowment Scholarship to Danielle Perry from UW Oshkosh, and 2015 Lina Vergara Memorial Scholarship to Michelle Xie from Middleton High School!

Lina's Story

Lina was only 20 years old when an accident took her life while she was rafting on the Wolf River. She loved life and nature, and she had a special passion for children. Lina wanted to become a third grade teacher, so after she graduated from Middleton High School, she enrolled at UW-Oshkosh and was about to start her junior year. Once she graduated, her goal was to teach kids and encourage them to build a better world.
