Event Information

Adults and children of all ages, from all over the world, will join together on May 10th, 2025, in memory of Lina Vergara to support the Lina Vergara Memorial Scholarship Fundraiser.

Registration for the 13th Annual Glowing Smiles 5K Run/Walk and Kids Dash will open on December 9th, 2024.

In the meantime, please check us out on Facebook or Instagram for updates and news on the upcoming race. Email us at glowingsmiles5k@gmail.com with any questions. We look forward to seeing you on race day!

Race Day

Be on time, so you don't miss all the fun!

Starting at 9:30 am, we will have awesome activities, including a raffle for the race participants, important announcements, refreshments, warm up exercises, face painting, balloon animals and the Kids Dash. Make sure you leave home early, so you can enjoy this day with your family and friends!

If you're curious about the route, check out the course map.

Schedule

  • 9:45 AM – Kids Dash
  • 10:00 AM – 5k Run/Walk

Awards

We will have medals and prizes for the top 3 male and 3 female finishers.

Cost

  • 15 years and older – $30
  • 14 years and younger – $15
  • Kids Dash – Free!

Lina's Story

Lina was only 20 years old when an accident took her life while she was rafting on the Wolf River. She loved life and nature, and she had a special passion for children. Lina wanted to become a third grade teacher, so after she graduated from Middleton High School, she enrolled at UW-Oshkosh and was about to start her junior year. Once she graduated, her goal was to teach kids and encourage them to build a better world.
